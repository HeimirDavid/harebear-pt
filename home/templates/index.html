About Me

Hi, my name is Neil McGill and I’m a Physical Trainer/Personal Coach, born and raised in Scotland and now enjoying life in Uppsala, Sweden.

My fitness journey started back in 2010 when I first joined the British Army. At that time I had only a basic knowledge of fitness, but during my four years serving I learned how to work with my body, to explore the limits of my ability and to push myself beyond my own expectations. I learned, also, how to motivate my peers; to help them raise their fitness and mental attitude to the elite standards expected by the military. Once I left the army to travel the length and breadth of Europe as a driver for international sporting events, I worked long hours, very often for weeks at a time, unable to access a gym for regular workouts. So, drawing upon my army experience and fitness knowledge, I began to use a combination of resistance bands and structured body-weight exercises to keep my fitness and muscle mass to the high levels I had come to expect. When I did visit a gym to test my muscle strength, I found I was making significant gains and was satisfied that the exercises I had devised to fit in with my busy lifestyle were producing the same effects as regular gym workouts.

I realise our modern lifestyle means it can be difficult for many of us to get to a gym these days, so if you find yourself in this situation - I can help. With my adapted hardcore army-style training, and encouraging, friendly attitude, I offer both group and individual workouts that are suitable for a range of abilities from absolute beginners to advanced levels. Whether you're a little Hare or a big Bear - if you want to learn how get fit with minimal equipment, I’d love to hear from you. I will work with you to design a training program tailor-made to suit your ability and fitness ambition and support you as you connect with your mind and body like never before.
