One on One

Group sessions are a great way to work out, but if you're really serious about maximising your fitness, the Hare Bear PT One-on-One training package could be for you. With my military background, recognised qualifications (Intermediate 2 Physical Education) and over 10 years’ experience in fitness and training, I can motivate and guide you in personal, one-to-one sessions.

What’s included?

  • An initial consultation where we discuss your current fitness, health routine and any injuries or problem areas.
  • A basic fitness test to determine your level.
  • Tailored workouts to help you reach your targets using minimal equipment and bodyweight routines.
  • Access to on-call fitness information when you need it.
  • Advice about nutritional support including meal suggestions and recipes.
  • Access to group training sessions included.

The benefits of rigorous fitness training are always enhanced by being outdoors; so, whenever possible, the lessons will take place in a natural outside area close to trees and green spaces. At the end of every lesson you will enjoy a relaxing cool-down with muscle stretches, rounding off the session with reflection, and reminders to show gratitude and appreciation for your body’s hard work.
